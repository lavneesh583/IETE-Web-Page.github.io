TIET, Patiala

IETE Students' Forum hosted at Thapar Insititute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala is the official electronics club of the university. The main objective of the society is to organize technical events that involve electronics, telecommunication, IT as a core area. Thus, infusing in the students the true essence of electronics. IETE provide a platform to exchange ideas and facilitates workshops and projects. The student mentors take care of the technical events of the society.
